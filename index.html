Carlos E. Bernal-Cárdenas

Senior Software Engineer Microsoft - Liquid Team

Carlos Eduardo Bernal-Cárdenas is a Senior Software Engineer at Microsoft on the Liquid team. He earned his Ph.D. in Computer Science in 2021 from William & Mary, advised by Dr. Denys Poshyvanyk, and holds a B.S. (2012) and M.Eng. (2015) from Universidad Nacional de Colombia. His research interests include software engineering, mining software repositories, and mobile app development and testing.
